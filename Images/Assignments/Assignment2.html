About Cwit

Cusrow Wadia Institute of Technology is situated in Pune in Maharashtra state of India. Established in 1938, it is accredited from AICTE Hostel facility is available for its students. Additional campus facilities such as Academic Zone, Auditorium, Boys Hostel, Computer Lab, Gym, Library, Medical Facilities, Cls. Room, Placement, Sports are also there.

About Computer dept

The Computer Department was started in 1984. Initially the following courses were conducted in the department : Post Diploma in Computer Application Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Applications Advanced Diploma in Computer Software, System Analysis & Applications The Autonomous Diploma Program in Computer Engineering was started in 1995. Well qualified and experienced faculty. Well equipped laboratories with all the required facilities and the equipments. Use of e-learning material. The computer centre of this Institute was the first centre to be established in Pune. Good alumni support is available in various activities like Guest Lectures, Industrial Visits, Curriculum Development etc.
